How should the orbs respond to touch?

The reference image says "Press on the canvas to focus and interact"

A

Ambient drift only

Orbs float and morph organically on their own — no touch interaction. Pure atmospheric background.

B

Touch-reactive

Orbs drift on their own, but respond to touch — they get attracted to or repelled from your finger, wobble on tap.

C

Draggable orbs

You can grab and drag orbs around the screen. They spring back or settle where you leave them.

D

Full interactive

Draggable + physics — orbs collide with each other, bounce off edges, and respond to device tilt (gyroscope).
